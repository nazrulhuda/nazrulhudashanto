Myself

Md. Nazrul Huda Shanto is a Graduate Research Assistant at Oklahoma State University, where he is pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science on a fully funded scholarship, maintaining a perfect GPA of 4.0/4.0. His current research focuses on leveraging AI to implement adaptive security in microservices. With over 11 months of experience as a Junior Software Engineer at Tirzok Private Ltd., Nazrul specialized in backend development. His work ranged from developing AI solutions and integrating AWS services to building backend systems using clients' preferred frameworks and databases. Notably, he implemented a document auto-validation system using machine learning and NLP for Kramasoft, a US-based mortgage borrower company. Additionally, Nazrul has over two years of experience as an Undergraduate Research Assistant at BRAC University, where he honed his skills in data collection and analysis. Nazrul is actively seeking a summer 2025 internship to further apply his skills and contribute to innovative projects.
