About

Myself

Welcome to my blog. I am Nazrul, nestled in the heart of Stillwater, Oklahoma, where I am completing my MS in Computer Science. A researcher at heart, with a tapestry of experience as a software engineer, I am now on the lookout for a Summer 2025 internship—a chance to bloom in new gardens. Beyond the halls of academia, I lose myself in the whispers of nature, hiking through trails unknown, and lending my voice to the stage’s embrace. The allure of acting and crafting cinematic tales draws me into realms of creativity and expression. I have never shied away from growth, even when the path seemed daunting. My vision is to leave an indelible mark with science, nurturing my surroundings with care. I believe that to truly care for my loved ones, I must first cultivate my own well-being. Thus, I am on a journey to become the best version of myself, each step a testament to my resolve and spirit.
